Mission

Rashtriya Yuva Vocational Training Saksharata Mission (RYVTSM) is a mission to provide computer education for all the people of India. This mission is initiated by Prem Bazar Swami Vivekananda Human Welfare Society (NGO) Regd. by West Bengal Govt & NCT (NEW DELHI) GOVT. OF INDIA. Our aims to spread the Mission every corner of our country by which every person will get better Computer Education and Training for their best future. We provide a good quality education and personality development for all. We are not working for profit but working for social service.

Prem Bazar Swami Vivekananda Human Welfare Society is a major Educational NGO in India with major presence in All State of India. Prem Bazar Swami Vivekananda Human Welfare Society (NGO) is dedicated to computer education for people of all class with special emphasis towards deprived and less fortunate people of our society.
