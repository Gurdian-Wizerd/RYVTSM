Placement

Placement is available to students under enrolled for Placement request application:
  1. The students should have successfully completed H.S. and must produce proof. of the same.
  2. The student pursuing Professional courses should fill up and submit the Placement Request Form latest by a month from the date of course completion. Registration charges for Placement Request is Rs. 400.00/- which is non refundable.
  3. Job will be provided to the students within 3 calendar months from the date of submission of placement request form.
  4. The student are required to exhibit the commitment and discipline as may be required for a professional work place. As such, students are expected to maintain high standard in attendance, punctuality, regularity etc. falling which enrollment with the Placement scheme may be cancelled on disciplinary grounds.
  5. The institute calls students for interview through phone, letter, fax or courier etc. If the student fails to attend the same within 48 hours then the institute does not take any liability in future for their placement.
  6. If any student fails to report on the date of appointment, then institute will not provide any other option under any circumstances nor can the candidate claim for the same.
  7. After filling the placement request form, if a student is found to reject the opportunity offered by the placement division due to personal reason, the institute does not take up any liability to provide the same again.
